Infrastructure & Network

Multimodal connectivity linking Mariel Port to Cuba's national infrastructure, Caribbean shipping lanes, and global trade routes

Connectivity

Multimodal Transport Hub

Mariel Port is supported by integrated land, rail, and air transport networks that connect the zone to domestic and international markets.

🚂

Rail Network

Dedicated freight rail corridor connecting port to national railway. Direct link to Havana's logistics center (45 km).

🛣

Road Access

Connected via Autopista Nacional (A1), Cuba's main highway. Dedicated port access road with 24/7 heavy vehicle capacity.

✈

Air Proximity

José Martí International Airport (HAV) 50 km east. Mariel Airstrip for charter/cargo operations under development.

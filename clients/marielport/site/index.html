Strategic Advantage

The Caribbean's Most Strategic Port

Positioned at the crossroads of Atlantic shipping lanes, Mariel offers unparalleled access to global markets with world-class infrastructure and investor-friendly policies.

⚓

Deep-Water Access

Accommodate Neo-Panamax vessels with 15-meter draft depth and a 700-meter berth operated by PSA International, one of the world's leading port operators.

🌐

Special Economic Zone

465 km² of designated development area with generous tax incentives, customs duty exemptions, and the ability for 100% foreign ownership of enterprises.

📊

Strategic Intelligence

Real-time port data, regulatory compliance monitoring, market analysis, and trade flow analytics to support informed investment decisions.

Investment Sectors

Diverse Opportunities Across Industries

ZED Mariel welcomes investment across multiple high-growth sectors, each supported by dedicated infrastructure and regulatory frameworks.

🏭

Manufacturing & Assembly

Duty-free import of raw materials and equipment with access to a skilled workforce and regional export markets.

💊

Biotechnology & Pharma

Leverage Cuba's world-renowned biotech sector and pharmaceutical R&D capabilities within a modern industrial zone.

🔋

Energy & Renewables

Solar, wind, and biomass projects supported by government incentives and growing demand for clean energy infrastructure.

📦

Logistics & Warehousing

Strategic transshipment hub connecting the Americas, with bonded warehousing and integrated customs facilities.

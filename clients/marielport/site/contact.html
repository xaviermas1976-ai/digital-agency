Common Questions

What information do you provide?

Comprehensive intelligence on Mariel Port operations, ZED Mariel investment framework, sector analysis, regulatory compliance, and shipping logistics.

Is ZED Mariel open to foreign investors?

Yes. ZED Mariel allows 100% foreign ownership of enterprises under Decree-Law 313. Over 62 businesses from 21 countries have been approved.

Contact our team for an initial consultation. We provide guidance on feasibility studies, application procedures, and regulatory requirements.

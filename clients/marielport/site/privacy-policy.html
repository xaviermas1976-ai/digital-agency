Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last Updated: May 2026

MarielPort (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website marielport.com and use our strategic intelligence platform.

1 Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide to us, as well as data gathered automatically when you access our platform:

  • Personal information submitted through contact forms, including your name, email address, company name, job title, and the content of your inquiry.
  • Analytics data collected automatically, including your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, referring URL, pages visited, time spent on pages, and other usage statistics.
  • Cookies and tracking technologies that help us understand how visitors interact with our platform.

2 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide requested intelligence reports.
  • Deliver tailored market analysis, port data, and investment intelligence.
  • Improve our platform, services, and user experience.
  • Send relevant communications about ZED Mariel developments, trade updates, and new reports—only when you have opted in to receive them.
  • Maintain the security and integrity of our platform.

3 Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3 (Transport Layer Security).
  • Personal data is stored on secure servers with strict access controls limited to authorized personnel only.
  • We conduct regular security assessments to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is never shared with external organizations except as described in this policy.

4 Cookies

Our platform uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience:

  • Essential cookies are required for core site functionality, including session management and security features. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics) help us understand traffic patterns, popular content, and user behavior. You may opt out of analytics tracking at any time by adjusting your browser settings or using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

5 Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — Request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion — Request that we delete your personal data, subject to any legal obligations requiring its retention.
  • Opt out — Unsubscribe from communications at any time by using the unsubscribe link in our emails or by contacting us directly.
  • Complaint — Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in your jurisdiction if you believe your data rights have been violated.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@marielport.com.

6 International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed in jurisdictions outside your country of residence. When we transfer data internationally, we ensure that adequate protection measures are in place in accordance with applicable data protection regulations, including contractual safeguards and security standards equivalent to those described in this policy.

7 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics — For collecting anonymized website usage statistics, including page views, session duration, and traffic sources. Google Analytics is governed by Google’s Privacy Policy.

We do not use third-party advertising services, and no personal data is shared with advertisers.

8 Data Retention

We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Contact form submissions are retained for 24 months from the date of submission, after which they are securely deleted.
  • Analytics data is retained for 14 months, in line with Google Analytics default retention settings.

If you request deletion of your data, we will process your request within 30 days.

9 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, or legal requirements. Any material changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

10 Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, requests, or concerns, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@marielport.com
  • Address: MarielPort, Mariel, Artemisa Province, Cuba

Questions About Your Data?

Our team is available to address any privacy concerns or data requests. We are committed to transparency and protecting your information.

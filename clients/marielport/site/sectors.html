Where Opportunity Meets Infrastructure

ZED Mariel welcomes investment across multiple strategic sectors aligned with Cuba's economic development priorities

Advanced Manufacturing

Light manufacturing, assembly operations, automotive parts, electronics. Cuba's labor costs 60% below regional average.

Biotechnology & Pharmaceuticals

Cuba's world-renowned biotech sector. Vaccines, monoclonal antibodies, biosimilars. 30+ research centers nearby.

Energy & Renewables

Solar, wind, biomass projects. Cuba targeting 37% renewable by 2030. Equipment manufacturing and assembly.

Tourism Infrastructure

Hotel construction, marina development, golf courses. Cuba received 1.2M tourists in 2023 with growth projected.

🌾

Agro-Industry & Food Processing

Food processing, packaging, agricultural technology. Import substitution priority for Cuban government.

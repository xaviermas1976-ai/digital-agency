## What Is This

APEX DIGITAL is a fully autonomous AI-powered marketing agency. It runs on [Claude Code](https://claude.ai/code) and orchestrates up to **112 specialized AI agents** across **16 divisions** to execute any digital marketing campaign — from SEO audits to full website builds to backlink campaigns — in minutes, not months.

**JARVIS** is the strategic brain layer. It remembers every client, every result, every learning. It plans the optimal strategy based on real historical data. It deploys APEX agents in parallel. It learns from every campaign and feeds those learnings into the next one.

Together, they form a self-improving marketing machine.

## Rules

1. **One brief = one deployment.** No unnecessary questions.
2. **Maximum parallelism.** Everything that can run simultaneously, does.
3. **Mandatory quality gate.** Nothing ships without Reality Checker + Evidence Collector.
4. **Data-driven.** Every recommendation backed by data, not opinions.
5. **JARVIS remembers.** Every decision, result, and client preference persists.
6. **Cross-pollination.** What works for one client feeds strategy for similar clients.
7. **Total autonomy.** JARVIS + APEX execute without asking for confirmation.
8. **Score threshold.** Deliverables below 80/100 get revised. Below 60, rejected and redone.
9. **Budget-aware.** Financial Analyst validates every allocation before execution.
10. **Learning loop.** Every campaign makes the next one better. Compounding intelligence.
